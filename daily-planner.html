Google Drive Setup Guide

Follow these steps once to get your Client ID. Takes about 10 minutes.

1 Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2 Click Select a project at the top, then New Project. Name it anything — e.g. "Daily Planner". Click Create.
3 In the left menu go to APIs & Services → Library. Search for Google Drive API and click Enable.
4 Go to APIs & Services → OAuth consent screen. Choose External, click Create. Fill in App name (e.g. "Daily Planner") and your email for support and developer contact. Click Save and Continue through all steps.
5 Go to APIs & Services → Credentials. Click + Create Credentials → OAuth client ID.
6 Set Application type to Web application. Under Authorised JavaScript origins click Add URI and enter the address you open this file from. If opening as a local file use null. Click Create.
7 Copy the Client ID shown (ends in .apps.googleusercontent.com). Paste it into the field above and tap Save & Connect.
8 The first time you connect, Google will show a warning: "Google hasn't verified this app." Click Advanced then Go to Daily Planner (unsafe). This is your own app — it is safe.
How sync works

Your data is saved as a single file called daily-planner-data.json in your Google Drive. The app reads from and writes to that file. You can see it in Drive at any time. Sync happens automatically when you make changes, and when you open the app.
